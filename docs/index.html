Project GW

From Digital to Pen & Paper

Project GW aims to deliver the world of Tyria, created for the original Guild Wars MMO,
to the Pen & Paper format to share the story with a wider audience.

learn more

D&D 5th Edition

The initial project will be built around Dungeons and Dragons 5th Edition - the most popular Pen & Paper RPG system.

Guild Wars Backbone

We are aiming to create the entire world presented in Guild Wars, allowing players to experience the world the way they want.

Open Source

This project is built with a heavy emphasis on keeping the entire project open and available to all - regardless what happens.

No Profits

Well, incorrect. Our profit is getting to play within Tyria and share the experience with anyone interested. Just no monetary gain.

Step One

World Building

The first step is to recreate the world of Tyria in a TTRPG format, requiring lots of description, care, and inspiration to deliver the feel of Tyria.

1
Step Two

Quest Seeds

Guild Wars gives some truly great lore - we want to help flavor the world while letting the Game Master add thier own flavor as well. Quest seeds will allow us to give some inspired direction to the Game Master without taking over the game. And yes - we plan to include the actual story too!

2
Step Three

Custom Classes

One of the things important to conveying the feeling of Guild Wars are the dual natured classes. D&D5e is built primarily for single classes, so we intend to work on custom classes to better balance out the feel and gameplay.

3
Step Four

Creature Creation

Guild Wars provided plenty of interesting creatures and variations of well known monsters. We plan on creating custom creatures to best represent those found in the world of Tyria, streamlined. This is going to be BIG.

4
Step Five

Presentation

Our design philosophy here to first get something useful, and then make put it in a format that makes people want to use it. When an area is finished, we plan to present it close to the presentation quality found within professionally published books.

5
The Future

There's plenty we can do when we finish the initial goal. Perhaps we work on other system conversions, or even start work converting Guild Wars 2.
